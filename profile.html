Biography

Professional Overview

Dr. K. Narayanan is a passionate academician with extensive experience in teaching, research, curriculum development and student mentoring. His commitment to quality education has inspired generations of learners while contributing significantly to institutional growth.

His areas of expertise include Commerce, Management, Business Research, Academic Administration and Research Methodology. Throughout his academic career, he has actively participated in conferences, faculty development programmes and scholarly research activities.

He strongly believes that education should empower individuals to think critically, innovate confidently and contribute meaningfully to society.
